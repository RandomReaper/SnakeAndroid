What is Snake ?:
This application is an adaptation of the snake game created during the first semester IT project of Industrial Systems at the HES-SO Valais-Wallis

What your data is used for ?:
The application uses your google account only to record your scores and achievements. No personal data is visible to the developers. 

What is the purpose of logging into your google account ?:
Through the connection with your google account, you can compare your scores and performances with your friends and other players.

Privacy Policy:
Only the connection status and the identification of your google account is stored by the application.
No personal data is shared. All data remains on your phone except your scores and achievements of course.
